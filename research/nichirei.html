Nichirei Corp (2871.T)

MARKET CAP: ¥432,330M | EXCHANGE: TSE | COMPOSITE: NIKKEI 225

Investment Thesis

Nichirei presents a high-margin safety play in the Japanese cold-chain sector. The core thesis rests on a fortress balance sheet where current assets nearly cover total liabilities, combined with a management team that utilizes aggressive buybacks (¥10B planned for 2025) to provide a valuation floor.
